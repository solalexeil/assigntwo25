About the JS Plugin

I used the fullPage.js plugin to create a digital photo album that feels more personal and immersive than just scrolling through a regular gallery. The full-screen design lets each photo or memory take center stage, making it easier for users to slow down and really experience the moments. It's a simple, intuitive way to bring the feeling of flipping through a real photo book to the web. This setup is perfect for sharing family memories, celebrating loved ones, or even honoring someone's life in a way that feels respectful and emotional. The scrolling transitions and full-page layout help guide people through the story without distractions, which makes it more meaningful.
